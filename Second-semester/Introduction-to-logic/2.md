## Deduction in Propositional Logic

# Natural Deduction system ND

$\frac{\Gamma \vdash \phi \lor \psi \quad \Gamma, \phi \vdash \delta \quad \Gamma, \psi \vdash \delta} {\Gamma \vdash \delta} \ (\lor E)$
$\frac{\Gamma \vdash \bot} {\Gamma \vdash \phi} \ (\bot E)$
$\frac{\Gamma, \phi \vdash \psi} {\Gamma \vdash \phi \to \psi} \ (\to I)$
$\frac{\Gamma \vdash \phi \to \psi \quad \Gamma \vdash \neg \phi} {\Gamma \vdash \psi} \ (\neg E)$
$\frac{\Gamma, \phi \vdash \bot} {\Gamma \vdash \neg \phi} \ (\neg I)$
