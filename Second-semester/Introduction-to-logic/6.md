## Proof-Checking with Explicit Substitutions

### Composition

(𝜏 • 𝜎) (𝑥) = 𝜎(𝑥) 𝜏 

**Note:** 𝜎 ⊑ 𝜏 if the substitution 𝜎 is more general than 𝜏
